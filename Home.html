A bird that can make you awe-struck, peacocks are the national bird of India. It has a long tail and has beautiful feathers, with which it dances. It’s a common belief in India, that a peacock dances for two reasons, one to attract her female counterpart, known as peahen and second it predicts rain, so it dances before it.
